there is and there are
-----------------------


countable and incountable nouns
-------------------------------

Los sustantivos contables son cosas que puedes contar con los dedos:
ejemplo:
    one onion, two onions, three onions...
    one tooth, two teeth, three teeth...

sustantivo no contables los usamos en cantidades indefinidas:
ejemplo:
    some water
    some rice
    some information

there is and there are with countable and uncountable nouns
-----------------------------------------------------------

usamos there is y there are para decir que algo existe.
usamos there is con sustantivos en singular y there are en sustantivos plurales

singular:
    there is one pear on the table.

plural:
    there are two pears on the table.


uncountable nouns
tambien usamos there is con sustantivos incontables

ejemplo:
    there is some milk in the fridge
    there's some tea in the teapot


there aren't any bananas today
there are no pears either
are there any oranges?
no, there aren't any
there are no mandarins either


nagative form
-------------
para formar la negativa usamos las contracciones de is not y are not:

with singular nouns:
    there isn't a piece of cake on the table

with plural :
    there aren't any eggs in the shop.

with uncountable nouns:
    there isn't any chocolate in the cupboard.

Fijate como se usa any con sustantivos incontables y en plural
there isn't any chocolate in the cupboard.

a veces se usa NO en lugar de ANY
